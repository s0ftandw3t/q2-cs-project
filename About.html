About the Founders

The founders of this advocacy, Aaron Jagorin, Ethan Sy, and Mico Nuqui are students of Philippine Science High School. They are currently in ninth grade.

Knowing firsthand the stress and the toll on mental health teenage life has, especially in an intense academic setting like PSHS, they decided that they would advocate for better access to mental health treatment to teens who need it. Knowing how widespread ICT is, especially among the younger generations, they decided that this would be their focus.
